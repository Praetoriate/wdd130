Beau is from Southern CA, doesn't really like to share his photo, and is a SCADA Specialist in the Water/Wastewater industry. Originally, he attended UCSD for Pre-Med and then served a mission in England (Leeds Mission). He is married, working full-time, and now studying... so no hobbies anymore.

These range from navigation types, colour schemes, content layouts, etc. A page (web or otherwise) can look really pretty, but at the end of the day is only as effective as it is intuitive. So in this course, Beau is looking to further expand this concept and take some new ideas back to work.
